Christmas at Old Westbury Gardens

DECEMBER 2017 ||| URL: Old Westbury Gardens' Calendar

Old Westbury Gardens features a beautiful estate filled with nature. It's about a seven minute drive from my house in Roslyn, but we didn't discover it until last summer. At the start, we reserved the library passes quite often to avoid paying the $12 admission fee per person. This year, we finally decided to purchase the yearly family membership.

Since the gardens are closed from January to April due to wintry conditions, we decided to visit in December. We were pleasantly surprised to find that in celebration of the holidays, the gardens set up a Christmas tree, played festive songs, and offered refreshments to visitors in the mansion. They had free cookies and apple cider!

On the way to the gardens, Old Westbury is home to many extravagant houses. There is an insurmountable sense of beauty to the area through its grand arrangement of trees and intricate architecture.
